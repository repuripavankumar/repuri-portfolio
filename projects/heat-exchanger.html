Heat Exchanger System Design

This academic project involved designing a shell-and-tube heat exchanger. The goal was to improve heat transfer performance while reducing material costs. Detailed drafting was done in AutoCAD, and the design incorporated dish head, baffle spacing, and optimized nozzle locations. The final system was presented with engineering drawings and a thermal analysis report.

  • Tools & Skills: AutoCAD · CATIA · 3D Modeling · Engineering Drawing · Heat Transfer Design
